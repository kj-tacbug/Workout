COURT DAY
INDOOR — TAP TO EXPAND
🔥
Warmup
10 min
▼
▶Jog baseline to baseline x3
▶Leg swings 2x10
▶Hip circles 2x10
▶Pogo jumps 2x10
▶Progressive shooting 5 min — no movement yet
🎯
Skill 1 — Shooting
15 min
▼
Spot Shooting
▶5 spots: L corner, L wing, top, R wing, R corner
▶3 attempts each spot
▶Sprint half court & back after every make
Pull Up Jumpers
▶Dribble from half court — pull up at free throw line
▶5 reps each side
▶Sprint baseline & back after every make
Midrange
▶Elbow jumpers left & right — 5 reps each
▶Sprint baseline & back after every make
🏀
Skill 2 — Handles & Finishing
15 min
▼
Full Court Dribble Finishes
▶Right hand only — layup
▶Left hand only — layup
▶Crossover series — floater
▶Between legs — reverse layup
▶3 reps each — walk back recovery
Floater Package
▶Right side x5 / Left side x5
▶Sprint half court & back after every make
Layup Package
▶Standard x5 each side
▶Euro step x5 each side
▶Reverse x5 each side
▶Sprint baseline & back after every make
💪
Conditioning
20 min
▼
Round 1 — Full Court Finishes
▶Dribble full court — finish strong
▶Sprint back immediately — no walking
▶Alternate hands each rep — 8 total reps
▶Rest 60 sec
Round 2 — Spot Shooting
▶Shoot from 5 spots
▶Make or miss — sprint half court & back
▶Repeat all 5 spots twice — no rest
Round 3 — 21
▶Every miss — sprint half court & back
▶Every 3 make — counts double, sprint full court & back
▶Finish the game no matter what
Round 4 — Final Burnout
▶Full court coast to coast x5 — max pace
▶Finish with layup or floater each time
▶Rest 90 sec — then 5 free throws to finish
🧘🏾
Cool Down
▼
▶Walk baseline to baseline x3
▶Hip flexor stretch 60 sec each side
▶Calf stretch 60 sec each side
▶Hamstring stretch 60 sec each side
▶Couch stretch 60 sec each side
▶Ankle rolls 2x10 each foot
🏆 Pre League — Before The 31st
▶Sprint intensity +10% each session
▶Add 2 extra full court finishes weekly
▶Track when you gas out — improve weekly
▶Free throws at end every session
▶Arrive 15 min early — always warm up first

TURF DAY
ZERO EQUIPMENT — TAP TO EXPAND
🔥
Warmup
10 min
▼
▶Light jog 3 min
▶Leg swings 2x10 each direction
▶Hip circles 2x10
▶Bodyweight squats 2x15
▶High knees 2x20 — fast and snappy
▶Butt kicks 2x20 — fast and snappy
▶Ankle bounces 2x20 — quick and light
⚡
Block 1 — Contrast Training
Fast Twitch Activator
▼
▶Reverse lunges 3x6 each leg — 3/1/1 tempo slow
▶Immediately into max effort vertical jump x3
▶Explode as fast as possible off the ground
▶Rest 2 min — repeat 3 rounds
▶Slow to fast contrast forces fast twitch to fire
🔁
Block 2 — Reactive Power
▼
▶Pogo jumps 4x15 — min ground contact, balls of feet only
▶Immediately into broad jumps 3x5 — max distance
▶Stick every landing 2 sec before moving
▶Rest 90 sec — repeat
🦵
Block 3 — Single Leg Power
▼
▶Speed bounds 3x8 each leg — aggressive single leg hops forward
▶Immediately into single leg glute bridge 3x12 each leg
▶Glute bridge tempo 3/1/1 — slow and controlled
▶Rest 90 sec — repeat
🚀
Block 4 — Vertical Jump Block
Max Effort
▼
Reactive Jump Squats
▶Drop fast into quarter squat
▶Immediately explode up — max height
▶Land soft — absorb 2 sec — reset
▶3x5 — rest 90 sec between sets
Approach Jumps
▶3x5 at 75-80% effort
▶Full arm swing — explode through the ground
▶Rest 2 min between sets
Standing Vertical Jumps
▶3x5 — maximum effort every single rep
▶Rest 2 min between sets
▶Never half effort — full power or don't do it
💨
Sprint Finisher
Fast Twitch Burnout
▼
▶10 yard sprints x4 — first step explosion
▶20 yard sprints x3 — full acceleration
▶Reactive sprint x3 — sprint 10 yards, shuffle 5, sprint back
▶Rest 90 sec between each
🧘🏾
Cool Down
▼
▶Walk 5 min easy
▶Hip flexor stretch 60 sec each side
▶Couch stretch 60 sec each side
▶Calf stretch 60 sec each side
▶Hamstring stretch 60 sec each side
▶Ankle rolls 2x10 each foot
▶Hip circles 2x10
🧠 Key Reminders
▶Max intent on every explosive movement — no half efforts
▶Pogos — ground contact as short as possible
▶Rest fully between vertical jump sets
▶Never do fast twitch work when fatigued
▶Block 1 contrast pairing — never skip it
